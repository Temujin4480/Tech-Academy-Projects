Kaewsa Resort

Kaewsa Community is in Mae Rim, Thailand, located about 20 kilometers north of Chiang Mai. Set amongst the backdrop of majestic mountains and stunning rice fields, Kaewsa has rooms for rent, houses for sale, available property, and is the home of Bangarang Muay Thai Boxing Gym
